Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 18, 2026

Lumina Style ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Lumina mobile application ("the App"). Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information from Apple Sign In

When you sign in with Apple, we may receive:

  • Apple User ID: A unique identifier for your account.
  • Name: Your first and last name (if you choose to share it).
  • Email: Your email address or an Apple-generated relay email (if you choose to share it).

1.2 Photos You Upload

When you use the virtual try-on feature, you upload photos from your device. These photos are:

  • Sent to our AI processing service solely to generate try-on results.
  • Not permanently stored on our servers after processing is complete.
  • Stored locally on your device as part of your Beauty History.

1.3 Usage Data

We may collect general usage data, including:

  • App interaction events (e.g., features used, styles selected).
  • Device information (e.g., device model, iOS version).
  • Crash reports and performance data.

1.4 Purchase Information

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store your payment information (credit card numbers, billing address, etc.). We only receive confirmation of successful transactions to credit Crystals to your account.

2. How We Use Your Information

Data Purpose
Apple User ID Account authentication and identification
Name & Email Personalization and account recovery
Uploaded Photos AI-powered virtual try-on processing
Usage Data App improvement, analytics, and bug fixing
Purchase Confirmations Crediting Crystals to your account

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • Apple ID credentials are stored securely in the iOS Keychain.
  • User preferences (login status, Crystal balance, settings) are stored in UserDefaults on your device.
  • Try-on history (generated images) is stored locally in an SQLite database on your device.
  • Chat history with the AI Beauty Advisor is stored in memory only and is not persisted between sessions.

3.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure Keychain storage for sensitive credentials.
  • HTTPS encryption for all network communications.
  • Minimal data collection principles — we only collect what is necessary.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • AI Processing: Photos are sent to our AI service provider for virtual try-on generation. These providers are contractually obligated to process data only as instructed and to maintain confidentiality.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Safety: We may disclose information to protect the rights, safety, or property of Lumina Style, our users, or the public.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Apple Sign In: For authentication (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy).
  • Apple StoreKit: For in-app purchases (governed by Apple's terms).
  • AI Image Processing API: For generating virtual try-on results.

6. Children's Privacy

Lumina is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data. You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the App's Settings page.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of certain data processing activities.

8. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will:

  • Remove your Apple ID credentials from the Keychain.
  • Clear your Crystal balance permanently.
  • Delete all Beauty History records and generated images.
  • Remove all locally stored user data.

This action is irreversible.

9. Data Retention

  • Uploaded photos: Deleted from processing servers immediately after AI generation is complete.
  • Local data: Retained on your device until you delete your account or uninstall the App.
  • Analytics data: Retained in anonymized form for up to 12 months.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
